TA (Teaching Assistants) Portal is desiged for Computer Science Department students, faculty and administration to streamline and make the hiring process easy. Students can login and submit applications to the Teaching Assistant positions for a subject, under a faculty for a semester. Students MUST indicate whether they've taken this course before, whether they've been TA before and upload a latest copy of resume for positions. At this moment, we are allowing only three TA applications for a student per semester.


Faculty may login and review the applications for their subjects. They can review and rate applications and recommend students. Faculty may also submit applications on behalf of potential students.


Admins from CCIS (College of Computer Science) may select the applications. Admins can easily create TA positions, update Faculty information, courses and semesters easily with a click of a button. They can also manage users, monitor site activity, block rogue users etc.
